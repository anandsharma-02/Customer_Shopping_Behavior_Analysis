# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior using a retail dataset. The objective is to extract meaningful insights related to sales performance, customer segmentation, product demand, and payment trends using interactive Power BI visualizations.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance and helps in data-driven decision-making.

## Objectives
* Identify the most and least purchased product categories
* Analyze shopping patterns across gender
* Evaluate preferred payment methods
* Understand sales trends over time
* Compare performance across different shopping malls

## Dashboard Features
###  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** 251.51M
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 299K
* **Total Profit:** 50.30M
* **Profit Margin Analysis**

### Visualizations Used
* **Bar Chart:** Quantity by Category
* **Stacked Bar Chart:** Sales by Gender & Category
* **Bar Chart:** Sales by Shopping Mall
* **Line Chart:** Sales Trend over Time
* **Donut Chart:** Sales by Payment Method

## Key Insights
### Product Category Analysis
* **Clothing** is the most purchased category
* **Books and Souvenirs** are the least purchased

### Customer Segmentation (Gender)
* Female customers contribute **higher total sales** compared to males
* Clothing and Cosmetics are more popular among female shoppers

### Payment Method Trends
* **Cash** is the most preferred payment method (~45%)
* Followed by **Credit Card (~35%)**
* **Debit Card (~20%)** is least used

### Shopping Mall Performance
* Top-performing malls include **Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon**
* Lower sales observed in smaller malls

### Time-Series Analysis

* Sales trend remains **consistent over time** with minor fluctuations
* Indicates stable customer demand

## Tools & Technologies
* **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard creation
* **Excel/CSV Dataset** – Data source
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI calculations

## Business Impact
* Helps businesses understand **customer preferences and buying behavior**
* Enables **targeted marketing strategies** based on gender and category
* Identifies opportunities to **promote digital payment adoption**
* Assists in **location-based performance optimization**

## Conclusion
The dashboard effectively transforms raw data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to make informed business decisions. It demonstrates strong analytical and visualization capabilities using Power BI.
